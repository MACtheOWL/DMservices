Swashbuckler Rogue

Feature Description
Fancy Footwork At 3rd level, when you make a melee attack against a creature, that creature can't make opportunity attacks against you for the rest of your turn.
Rakish Audacity Starting at 3rd level, you gain a bonus to initiative rolls equal to your Charisma modifier.

Panache At 9th level, your Persuasion becomes exceptionally effective:

- Against Hostile Creatures: As an action, make a Persuasion check contested by their Insight. On success, they have disadvantage on attack rolls against others and can't make opportunity attacks against anyone but you for 1 minute, unless one of your allies attacks them or casts a spell on them.
- Against Non-Hostile Creatures: On a successful check, they are charmed for 1 minute and consider you a friendly acquaintance. This ends if you or an ally harm them.
Elegant Maneuver At 13th level, you can use a bonus action to gain advantage on your next Dexterity (Acrobatics) or Strength (Athletics) check made during the same turn.
Master Duelist At 17th level, if you miss with an attack roll, you can reroll the attack with advantage. Once used, you can't use this feature again until you finish a short or long rest.
