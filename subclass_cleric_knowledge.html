Class Features

Feature Description
Blessings of Knowledge At 1st level, you learn two languages of your choice. You also gain proficiency in two skills from Arcana, History, Nature, or Religion. Your proficiency bonus is doubled for checks using those skills.
Channel Divinity: Knowledge of the Ages At 2nd level, you can use Channel Divinity to gain proficiency with a chosen skill or tool for 10 minutes.
Channel Divinity: Read Thoughts At 6th level, you can read a creature's surface thoughts. If the creature fails a Wisdom saving throw, you can also cast Suggestion on it without expending a spell slot.
Potent Spellcasting At 8th level, you add your Wisdom modifier to the damage dealt with any cleric cantrip.
Visions of the Past At 17th level, you can meditate on an object or area to gain visions of past events. These visions can reveal information about an object's previous owner or significant events in a location.
