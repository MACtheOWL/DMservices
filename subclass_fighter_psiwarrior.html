Psi Warrior Fighter

Feature Description
Psionic Power At 3rd level, you harbor a wellspring of psionic energy within yourself. This energy is represented by your Psionic Energy dice, which are each a d6. You have a number of these dice equal to twice your proficiency bonus, and they fuel various psionic powers you have.
Protective Field When you or another creature you can see within 30 feet of you takes damage, you can use your reaction to expend one Psionic Energy die, roll the die, and reduce the damage taken by the number rolled plus your Intelligence modifier (minimum reduction of 1), as you create a momentary shield of telekinetic force.
Psionic Strike Once on each of your turns, immediately after you hit a target within 30 feet of you with an attack and deal damage to it with a weapon, you can expend one Psionic Energy die, rolling it and dealing force damage to the target equal to the number rolled plus your Intelligence modifier.
Telekinetic Movement You can move an object or a creature with your mind. As an action, you target one loose object that is Large or smaller or one willing creature. If you can see the target and it is within 30 feet of you, you can move it up to 30 feet to an unoccupied space you can see.
Telekinetic Adept At 7th level, you have mastered new ways to use your telekinetic abilities. This includes Psi-Powered Leap (gain flying speed) and Telekinetic Thrust (force a Strength saving throw to knock a target prone or move it).
Guarded Mind Starting at 10th level, you gain resistance to psychic damage and can expend a Psionic Energy die to end the charmed or frightened condition on yourself.
Bulwark of Force At 15th level, you can shield yourself and others with telekinetic force. As a bonus action, you can protect creatures within 30 feet with half cover for 1 minute or until you're incapacitated.
Telekinetic Master By 18th level, you can cast the Telekinesis spell without components, using Intelligence for spellcasting. You also gain a bonus weapon attack each turn while concentrating on the spell.
