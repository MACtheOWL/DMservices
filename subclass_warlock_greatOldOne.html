Class Features

Feature Description
Awakened Mind Starting at 1st level, you can telepathically speak to any creature within 30 feet of you that you can see. The creature does not need to share a language with you, but it must understand at least one language to comprehend your thoughts.
Entropic Ward At 6th level, when a creature makes an attack roll against you, you can use your reaction to impose disadvantage on the attack roll. If the attack misses you, your next attack roll against the creature has advantage if made before the end of your next turn. Once you use this feature, you can't use it again until you finish a short or long rest.

Create Thrall At 14th level, you can use your action to touch an incapacitated humanoid. That creature becomes charmed by you until a Remove Curse spell is cast on it, the charmed condition is removed, or you use this feature again. You can communicate telepathically with the charmed creature as long as you are on the same plane of existence.
