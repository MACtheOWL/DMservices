Class Features

Feature Description
Among the Dead At 1st level, you learn the Spare the Dying cantrip, which counts as a warlock cantrip for you. You also gain advantage on saving throws against disease. Undead creatures targeting you with an attack or harmful spell must make a Wisdom saving throw against your spell save DC. On a failed save, they must choose a new target or waste their attack/spell. This effect lasts for 24 hours. The effect ends early if you or the undead target each other with harmful actions.
Defy Death At 6th level, you can regain hit points equal to 1d8 + your Constitution modifier (minimum 1 hit point) when you succeed on a death saving throw or stabilize a creature with Spare the Dying. This feature can only be used once per long rest.
Undying Nature At 10th level, you no longer need to eat, drink, or sleep, though you still require rest to reduce exhaustion. Additionally, you age slower, aging only 1 year for every 10 years that pass, and you are immune to magical aging.
Indestructible Life At 14th level, you can use a bonus action to regain hit points equal to 1d8 + your warlock level. If you reattach a severed body part during this feature's use, the part will reattach. This feature can only be used once per short or long rest.
