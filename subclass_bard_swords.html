College of Swords Bard

Name College of Swords Bard Features
Bonus Proficiencies You gain proficiency with medium armor and scimitars.
You can use any simple or martial melee weapon you are proficient with as a spellcasting focus for your bard spells.
Fighting Style Choose one of the following fighting styles:
Dueling: +2 bonus to damage rolls when wielding a melee weapon in one hand and no other weapons.
Two-Weapon Fighting: Add your ability modifier to the damage of the second attack in two-weapon fighting.
Blade Flourish Whenever you take the Attack action, your walking speed increases by 10 feet until the end of the turn.
If you hit a creature with a weapon attack as part of this action, you can use one of the following Blade Flourish options (once per turn):
Defensive Flourish Expend a Bardic Inspiration die to deal extra damage equal to the die roll. Add the same number to your AC until the start of your next turn.
Slashing Flourish Expend a Bardic Inspiration die to deal extra damage equal to the die roll to both your target and another creature within 5 feet.
Mobile Flourish Expend a Bardic Inspiration die to deal extra damage equal to the die roll. Push the target 5 feet plus the die roll. You may then use your reaction to move up to your walking speed to an unoccupied space within 5 feet of the target.
Extra Attack Starting at 6th level, you can attack twice whenever you take the Attack action on your turn.
Master's Flourish Starting at 14th level, when you use a Blade Flourish, you can roll a d6 instead of expending a Bardic Inspiration die.
