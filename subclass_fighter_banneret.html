Banneret Fighter Features

Feature Description
Rallying Cry When you use your Second Wind feature, you can choose up to three creatures within 60 feet of you to regain hit points equal to your fighter level, provided they can see or hear you.
Royal Envoy At 7th level, you gain proficiency in the Persuasion skill. If you are already proficient in it, you can choose one of the following skills to gain proficiency: Animal Handling, Insight, Intimidation, or Performance. Your proficiency bonus is doubled for any ability check you make using Persuasion.
Inspiring Surge At 10th level, when you use Action Surge, one allied creature within 60 feet can make a melee or ranged weapon attack with its reaction, provided they can see or hear you. At 18th level, you can choose two allies instead of one.
Bulwark At 15th level, when you use your Indomitable feature to reroll an Intelligence, Wisdom, or Charisma saving throw, you can extend the benefit to one ally within 60 feet who also failed the same saving throw. If they can see or hear you, they can reroll the saving throw and must use the new result.
