Class Features

Feature Description
Channel Divinity: Conquering Presence At 3rd level, you can exude a terrifying presence, forcing creatures to make a Wisdom saving throw. Failed saves result in being frightened for 1 minute.
Channel Divinity: Guided Strike At 3rd level, you can use Channel Divinity to gain a +10 bonus to an attack roll after seeing the roll but before the DM announces the result.
Aura of Conquest At 7th level, creatures frightened by you have their speed reduced to 0 and take psychic damage equal to half your paladin level. The aura extends 10 feet.
Scornful Rebuke At 15th level, when a creature hits you with an attack, it takes psychic damage equal to your Charisma modifier (minimum of 1).
Invincible Conqueror At 20th level, you can become an avatar of conquest for 1 minute, gaining resistance to all damage, an extra attack on your turn, and critical hits on rolls of 19 or 20.
