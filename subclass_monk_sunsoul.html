Way of the Sun Soul Monk

Feature Description
Radiant Sun Bolt At 3rd level, you gain a new ranged attack option. This special attack is a ranged spell attack with a 30-foot range, dealing radiant damage equal to a d4 + your Dexterity modifier. The damage die increases as you gain monk levels. When you take the Attack action and use this attack, you can spend 1 ki point to make two additional attacks as a bonus action. This attack can also be used with the Extra Attack feature.
Searing Arc Strike At 6th level, after taking the Attack action, you can spend 2 ki points to cast Burning Hands as a bonus action. You can increase the spell level by spending additional ki points, up to half your monk level.
Searing Sunburst At 11th level, as an action, you hurl an orb of radiant light up to 150 feet. It explodes in a 20-foot-radius sphere. Creatures in the area must make a Constitution saving throw or take 2d6 radiant damage. You can increase the damage by spending up to 3 ki points, each adding 2d6 extra damage.
Sun Shield At 17th level, you radiate bright light in a 30-foot radius and dim light for another 30 feet. You can extinguish or restore this light as a bonus action. If a creature hits you with a melee attack while this light shines, you can use your reaction to deal radiant damage equal to 5 + your Wisdom modifier.
