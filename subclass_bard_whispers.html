College of Whispers Bard

Name College of Whispers Bard Features
Psychic Blades When you hit a creature with a weapon attack, expend one use of your Bardic Inspiration to deal an additional 2d6 psychic damage. The damage increases at higher levels: 3d6 at 5th level, 5d6 at 10th level, and 8d6 at 15th level. You can do this once per round.
Words of Terror If you speak to a humanoid for 1 minute, the target must succeed on a Wisdom saving throw or be frightened of you (or another creature) for 1 hour, or until it is attacked or damaged. If it succeeds, it remains unaware of your attempt.
Mantle of Whispers When a humanoid dies within 30 feet of you, you can capture its shadow and disguise yourself as the person. This lasts for 1 hour or until you end it as a bonus action. You gain information about the person and can pass yourself off as them. A Wisdom (Insight) check contests your Charisma (Deception) check to see through the disguise.
Shadow Lore As an action, you whisper a phrase to a creature within 30 feet, causing it to make a Wisdom saving throw.
