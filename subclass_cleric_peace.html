Class Features

Feature Description
Implement of Peace At 1st level, you gain proficiency in the Insight, Performance, or Persuasion skill (your choice).
Emboldening Bond At 1st level, you can forge a magical bond among creatures at peace. You create a magical bond for 10 minutes or until you use this feature again. Bonded creatures can add a d4 to attack rolls, ability checks, or saving throws when within 30 feet of each other.
Channel Divinity: Balm of Peace At 2nd level, you can use your Channel Divinity to move up to your speed without provoking opportunity attacks. You can heal creatures within 5 feet of you for 2d6 + Wisdom modifier hit points (minimum of 1 hit point). A creature can only be healed once during this movement.
Protective Bond At 6th level, when a creature affected by your Emboldening Bond is about to take damage, a bonded creature within 30 feet can use its reaction to teleport and take the damage instead.
Potent Spellcasting At 8th level, you add your Wisdom modifier to the damage you deal with any cleric cantrip.
Expansive Bond At 17th level, the range of your Emboldening Bond and Protective Bond features increases to 60 feet. Additionally, when a creature uses Protective Bond to take damage, the creature gains resistance to that damage.
