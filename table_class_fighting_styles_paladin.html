Paladin Styles Description
Defense While you are wearing armor, you gain a +1 bonus to AC.
Dueling When you are wielding a melee weapon in one hand and no other weapons, you gain a +2 bonus to damage rolls with that weapon.
Great Weapon Fighting When you roll a 1 or 2 on a damage die for a melee weapon wielded with two hands, reroll and use the new result. Weapon must have the two-handed or versatile property.
Protection When a creature you can see attacks a target other than you within 5 feet, use your reaction to impose disadvantage. You must be wielding a shield.
Blessed Warrior (TCE) You learn two cleric cantrips. Charisma is your spellcasting ability. You can replace one when gaining a level in this class.
