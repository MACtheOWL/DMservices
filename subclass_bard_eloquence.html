Name College of Eloquence Bard Features
Silver Tongue You have a way with words. When you make a Charisma (Persuasion) or Charisma (Deception) check, you can treat a d20 roll of 9 or lower as a 10.
Unsettling Words As a bonus action, you can expend one use of Bardic Inspiration to choose a creature within 60 feet. Roll your Bardic Inspiration die, and the creature must subtract the number rolled from the next saving throw it makes before the start of your next turn.
Unfailing Inspiration When a creature adds one of your Bardic Inspiration dice to an ability check, attack roll, or saving throw and still fails, the creature does not lose the Bardic Inspiration dieâ€”it can keep it and use it again.
Universal Speech As an action, you can choose a number of creatures within 60 feet equal to your Charisma modifier (minimum of one). For 1 hour, those creatures can understand you, regardless of the language you speak.
This feature recharges after a long rest, or you can expend a spell slot to use it again.
Infectious Inspiration When a creature within 60 feet of you successfully uses one of your Bardic Inspiration dice, you can use your reaction to inspire another creature (other than yourself) that can hear you, granting it a Bardic Inspiration die without expending a use.
You can use this reaction a number of times equal to your Charisma modifier (minimum of once) per long rest.
