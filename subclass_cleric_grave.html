Class Features

Feature Description
Circle of Mortality At 1st level, when you restore hit points to a creature at 0 hit points, you use the highest number possible for each die. You also gain the Spare the Dying cantrip with a range of 30 feet, and you can cast it as a bonus action.
Eyes of the Grave At 1st level, you can detect the presence of undead within 60 feet. This ability can be used a number of times equal to your Wisdom modifier (minimum of once), regaining expended uses after a long rest.
Channel Divinity: Path to the Grave Starting at 2nd level, you can curse a creature within 30 feet. The next time you or an ally hits the cursed creature with an attack, it becomes vulnerable to all of that attack's damage, and the curse ends.
Sentinel at Death's Door At 6th level, you can use your reaction to turn a critical hit on you or an ally within 30 feet into a normal hit. Effects triggered by a critical hit are canceled. This can be used a number of times equal to your Wisdom modifier (minimum of once), regaining expended uses after a long rest.
Potent Spellcasting At 8th level, you add your Wisdom modifier to the damage dealt with any cleric cantrip.
